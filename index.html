Have you watched The Royal Hotel?

It is a 2023 Australian psychological thriller directed by Kitty Green, starring Julia Garner, Jessica Henwick, and Hugo Weaving. The story follows two best friends backpacking in Australia who run out of money and take a live-in bartending job at a remote outback pub called The Royal Hotel. What starts as a way to earn some cash quickly turns into an unsettling situation as the locals' behavior begins to cross the line. The film is inspired by the 2016 documentary Hotel Coolgardie by Pete Gleeson.

Have you watched Blink Twice?

It is a 2024 American psychological thriller directed by Zoë Kravitz in her feature directorial debut, starring Naomi Ackie and Channing Tatum. The story follows a cocktail waitress named Frida who gets invited by tech billionaire Slater King to his private island for a dream vacation — but as strange things begin to happen, she starts to question her reality and must uncover the truth to survive. If you have been following the Epstein files and find yourself troubled by stories of powerful men and their private islands, this movie will feel uncomfortably familiar — it taps into that same unsettling world of wealth, abuse, and silence. Note that the film contains mature themes including depictions of violence and sexual violence.
