Have you watched The Royal Hotel?

Poetry

People are Like Fungus

we are like fungus,
we grow around them
as people gets to know us.

from a dot of possibilities
to a bundle of disgust !

when the spot's all damp and green
we try to fix the scene
we smother them,
suffocate them,
make them weep !

collect their tears
and use them to sweep

when the spot's all clean and shiny
we wonder
how could it hurt so much
something so, so tiny !

only after they're gone
we realize the marks they leave
to get them back
what wouldn't we give !

from a dot of possibilities
to a bundle of disgust !

we loathe ourselves
we can't
clean memories of the past

something so damp
something so green
something so sick
makes us so hopeless
powerless
a two-faced prick !

we are like fungus.
a little rain
a little self-loathing
and a little past
always brings the best in us!

— 3:45 am, 30th November 2016
